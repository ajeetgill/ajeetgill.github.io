Detail, what do I mean in the tweet?

Most often, we hear people saying, you should be polite to others, kind and what not. We don't like when someone says something to us, and it hurts us, and we blame the next person for disturbing our "inner peace." Lecture how they should be more sensitive towards someone's feelings. To which I say, are you that fragile? Is your inner peace that fragile? Have thick skin, be confident in yourself, know yourself, embrace the good, the bad, the weaknesses, and the strengths. By doing so, what others say will, at one point, stop affecting you. You'll know that the next person's opinion is from an ill-informed place with not as much information about you and your circumstances. Stop letting anyone's mere words affect your mood/inner peace.

Example: the 'but how?' part.

Next time you feel offended, interrupt yourself and ask,

Did this thing cause me real physical harm?

or just HMU on any of the social media. Let's talk :)
